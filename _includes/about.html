About

This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. The journey began with a great passion for democratizing access to financial information. As a team of financial experts, tech enthusiasts, and industry professionals, we recognized the need for a tool that would empower both seasoned investors and newcomers to navigate the complexities of the stock market.

Our mission is to democratize access to financial data. We believe that everyone, from seasoned investors to first-time traders, deserves a user-friendly and reliable tool to navigate the complexities of the stock market!!
